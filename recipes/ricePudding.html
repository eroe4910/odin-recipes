Easy Rice Pudding Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste substitute vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup golden raisins optional

Steps

  1. Combine the milk, water, sugar, cinnamon stick and rice. Stir to combine.
  2. Bring to an intense simmer, stirring often. Reduce heat.
  3. Simmer for 35 to 45 minutes, until the rice is tender.
  4. Stir in vanilla and raisins.
  5. Transfer the pudding into a serving container, cover tightly and cool.
